# Custom Algorithm Builder (CAB): The Revolutionary Feature

## Why This Changes Everything

**OpenSignals' Custom Algorithm Builder is Steve Jobs-level innovation** - it's not just a feature, it's a fundamental reimagining of how trust scoring works in advertising.

## The Problem We're Solving

Every existing solution (AdCP, AAMP, competitors) has the same fatal flaw: **one-size-fits-all trust scoring**.

- A luxury automotive brand has VERY different risk tolerance than a DTC startup
- A pharmaceutical company needs 100% compliance, a gaming company prioritizes performance
- A privacy-first brand cares about data provenance, a growth-stage company wants reach
- **Yet everyone gets the same scoring algorithm**

This is like giving everyone the same investment strategy regardless of their risk tolerance, age, or goals. It's absurd.

## The Revolution: Personalized Trust

CAB allows every brand to define their own trust scoring algorithm by:

1. **Describing what they want in plain English** - No code, no configuration files, no technical knowledge required
2. **Learning from their behavior** - The system observes your decisions and adapts
3. **Evolving over time** - As campaigns succeed or fail, the algorithm improves
4. **Sharing with the community** - Discover how other brands approach trust
